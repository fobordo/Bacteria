Project Overview:

The purpose of the Belly Button Biodiversity analysis was to observe bacterial species that have ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. It is hypothesized that the ideal bacterial species to make synthetic beef may be found in the belly button. To test this hypothesis, the navels of people across the country were sampled to identify bacterial species that colonize in human belly buttons. Each person's identity is anonymous, and are instead assigned an ID number. Select a Test Subject ID Number from the dropwdown menu below to see which bacterial species live in each person's naval.
